Marble Rooster Transport.

We speak your language and we understand how to handle your freight to treat it like our precious.

Applied Logistics has, over the years, moved a great many sculptures and works of art. We have a specialist team who is responsible for packing and crating these special pieces. As you can see, every care is being taken. Below, you will see images of two fighting marble roosters, perched atop a marble column. These were packed and cased suitable for rail transport from Melbourne to Brisbane. Our customer was delighted with the work performed and insisted on rail freight as it gives the smoothest ride.

We were proud to receive the below comments from the client:

“Most efficient. Thank Riki very much for that – from the outside it looks like he packed the Ark of the Covenant.
Do we return the straps to anyone once the Roosters are unpacked?

Regards,
Margaret”
